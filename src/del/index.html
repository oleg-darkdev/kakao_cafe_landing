About Us

At GreenLeaf Café, we are committed to serving you the freshest, most delicious meals made from organic and locally-sourced ingredients.

We also pride ourselves on our craft-brewed Fairtrade coffee drinks, made with beans from local Portland roastery Sterling Coffee Roasters.

Our mission is to provide a warm and inviting space where you can enjoy healthy, flavorful food that is good for you and the environment.
